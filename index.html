My Community Map

My name is Bennett Lovejoy and I am a data-driven policy analyst.

I made this website so that young scholars, teachers, administrators, citizens, researchers, and policymakers alike can interact with data about their community.

The first part of this project focused on economic and social indicators of community wellness.

On the map below you can click between various layers of the map to see data about Ohio.

This data was obtained from the:

  • National Center for Education Statistics
  • United States Census Bureau
